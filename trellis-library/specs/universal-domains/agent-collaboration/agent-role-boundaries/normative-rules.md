# Normative Rules

* Multi-agent work must define who coordinates, who executes, who reviews, and where human approval remains required.
* Agents must not silently expand their authority beyond the role assigned for the current task.
* Responsibility boundaries should minimize duplicated work and conflicting edits.
* High-risk decisions must remain attributable to an explicit decision owner rather than diffusing across multiple agents.
* When role boundaries are unclear, the work should be treated as coordination risk rather than assumed to be safe.
