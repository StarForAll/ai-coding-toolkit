# Normative Rules

* Incident handling must prioritize containment, user impact reduction, and restoration of safe operation over speculative root-cause debate.
* Roles, decision authority, and communication paths must become explicit once incident handling begins.
* Temporary mitigations must be distinguishable from durable fixes.
* Evidence gathered during response should support later diagnosis without slowing immediate containment.
* When impact, scope, or recovery status is unclear, uncertainty must be communicated rather than masked.
