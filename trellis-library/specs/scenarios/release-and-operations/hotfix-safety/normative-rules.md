# Normative Rules

* A hotfix must remain narrowly scoped to the urgent problem unless expanded scope is explicitly justified.
* Time pressure must not erase the need for truthful verification boundaries and rollback readiness.
* Temporary mitigations shipped as hotfixes must be distinguishable from complete corrective fixes.
* Hotfix decisions should reflect blast radius, reversibility, and current uncertainty rather than urgency alone.
* Deferred cleanup or follow-up work created by the hotfix must remain visible after the immediate incident passes.
