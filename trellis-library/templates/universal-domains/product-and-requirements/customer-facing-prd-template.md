# Customer-Facing PRD Template

## Purpose

Use this template to create customer-facing Product Requirement Documents (PRDs) that are clear, business-focused, and suitable for non-technical stakeholders. This template ensures all necessary elements are included for effective stakeholder communication and decision-making.

## Instructions

1. **Fill in all sections**: Complete each section with specific information
2. **Use business language**: Avoid technical jargon and focus on business value
3. **Be specific**: Use measurable terms and clear success criteria
4. **Review with stakeholders**: Have business stakeholders review for clarity
5. **Update as needed**: Revise the document as requirements evolve
